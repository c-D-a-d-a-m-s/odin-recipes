Grilled Cheese

Description

This childhood classic is filled with ooey gooey goodness. This easy recipe is a staple in our household.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices - Sandwich bread
  • 1 slice - American cheese
  • 1/2 tbsp - Butter

Steps

  1. Heat up a small frying pan over medium heat. (A griddle set to 300°F can be used for larger batches.
  2. Coat pan with 1/4 tbps of butter. Then, place the first slice of bread in pan.
  3. Place cheese slice on bread. Then, place second slice on top.
  4. Use a spatula to smash the sandwich down. Let sit for about two minutes, or until bottom of the sandwich is golden brown.
  5. Flip the sandwich and wait until second slice is also golden brown.
  6. Remove the sandwich from the pan and serve with favorite chips.
