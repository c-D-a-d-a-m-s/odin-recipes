Fried Egg

Description

A simple breakfast to start the day. Fried eggs with toast is the go-to breakfast for our household.

Ingredients

  • 1 - Egg
  • 2 tsp - Olive oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Bring a small frying pan to medium heat on stovetop.
  2. Coat bottom of pan with olive oil.
  3. Crack egg into pan and let cook until egg whites are solid.
  4. Flip the egg and cook another half minute.
  5. Remove from pan and salt and pepper to taste.
