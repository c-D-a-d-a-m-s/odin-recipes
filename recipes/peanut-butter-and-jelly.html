Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

From wartime to playtime, who can resist this scrumptious sandwich. This remains the go-to on-the-go lunch in our house.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices - Sandwich bread
  • Peanut butter (Crunchy or smooth)
  • Jelly (Choose your favorite. My household prefers strawberry.)

Steps

  1. Using a knife or spoon, scoop out a hefty helping of peanut butter and spread it over the first slice of bread.
  2. If you have the squeezable jelly, you can squeeze a decent dollop onto the second slice of bread and use the same utensil from step 1 to spread the jelly. Otherwise, use a clean spoon to scoop out jelly onto the second slice of bread and spread.
  3. Now, merry the two slices of bread with the peanut butter- and jelly-sides facing one another, and serve with your favorite chips.
